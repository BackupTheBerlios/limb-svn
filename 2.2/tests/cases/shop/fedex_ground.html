  • The rate quote shown may be different than the actual charges for your shipment. Differences may occur based on actual weight, dimensions, and/or address correction. Consult the most recent FedEx Service Guide or your FedEx pricing agreement for details on how shipping charges are calculated.
  • The displayed rates apply to dropoff shipments at any of the FedEx drop boxes or staffed FedEx World Service Centers.
  • FedEx Express Hundredweight pricing is available for multiple piece shipments traveling on a single FedEx Airbill to one destination, on the same day, and totaling 100 lbs. or more.
  • FedEx Ground Multiweight is available for multiple packages going to the same postal code on the same day, weighing 150 lbs. or more.
  • You must be registered for FedEx Ground service to use Ground International to Canada and Puerto Rico when shipping from a FedEx World Service Center.

  • FedEx Ground will provide pickup service to business locations, upon request, for a Weekly Service Charge of $7. An additional charge of $4 will apply to shippers who ship less than $60 in weekly package charges.
